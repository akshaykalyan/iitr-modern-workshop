The Modern Workshop is a common facility which provides fabrication services for projects and prototype fabrication to all the students and faculty members of the Institute. The vision, scope and mission of the proposed modern workshop are as mentioned below.

VISION

Act as a resource base for technically sound and trained engineers.
Attain excellence and become a unique Institution.
Become an incubation Centre for design, planning and realizing the innovative ideas generated by young engineering students.
To impart quality education to achieve excellence in teaching learning and research.
To provide skill oriented training to meet the need of the industry and society.
To undertake collaborative projects with academia and industries.

SCOPE

In order to attain the visions, modern workshop being used mainly for the following activities :
To provide a good theoretical background as well as a sound practical on hand practice to students to impart basic knowledge of different Manufacturing Processes and properties of different material used in industry and to appraise the students of basics and applications of various types of machines-tools and equipment used in different shops such as Machine Shop, Fitting Shop, Foundry Shop, Smithy Shop, Welding Shop, Carpentry Shop and Electric Repair & Maintenance Shop of Mechanical Workshop.
Each Shop has relevant charts, Lab. manuals, demonstration board and operations which help in imparting quality education to students.
Fabrication of Projects/Samples/Test Pieces of B.Tech. , M.Tech. and Research students, Sponsored Projects, Faculty Research Projects and Laboratory equipment etc.
Miscellaneous work of non-academic sections of the Institute.
To conduct some short term training programs for participants of Institute and outside.

MISSION

Acquiring most modern Machine-tools and equipment to transform existing inadequate and age-old technical support and educational facilities, and designing the training programs for student and Workshop staff to meet the requirement of the teaching, practice and research would also help in providing the necessary support and consultancy to the industries.
To develop and include more Shops and facilities such as Electric Shop, FRP Shop etc. with the aim to provide maximum latest technical exposure to students.
To upgrade and make it a central facility of the Institute.
To make available each & everything regarding Workshop on IITR website.
To make online work processing and monitoring system.
